About Us.

Group of stategist who are dedicated to exeeding exepectations​
​Driven by passion, created by top level talent, and a team that is musically inclined + business savvy; Ep Sounds provides music managers, labels, and artists of any size access to tier-1 distribution, licensing, and music marketing opportunities. We are independent music distribution and licensing platform. We’ve evened the playing field for everyone to be able to compete with the majors at affordable rates and unsurpassed customer care.

OUR MISSION

We’re a family here, and once you join – you’re a part of it too. Our mission is to give our family of content creators all the tools, tech, resources, and outreach to maximize their music career and budgets at every step.

OUR VISION

We.Don’t.Stop. We don’t stop building out our platform, features, lines of communication, tool development, end user experience, one on one support, all to help our family succeed faster.

WHY WE'RE DIFFRENT
